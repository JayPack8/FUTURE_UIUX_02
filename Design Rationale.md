## Design Rationale - **MamaCare**



### **Target Users**



***MamaCare*** is designed primarily for pregnant women, especially first-time mothers aged 20–35 who need structured, reliable, and reassuring support throughout their pregnancy journey. These users often experience uncertainty around scheduling checkups, tracking progress, and remembering important dates.



Secondary users include healthcare providers such as gynecologists and clinic staff who manage appointments and patient interactions.



### **Booking Flow Logic**



\-> Users begin by exploring the app without mandatory sign-up



\-> Pregnancy week is captured during onboarding to personalize recommendations



\-> users can start booking by clicking on the book button



\-> Users select available Hospitals or Clinics



\-> Users select available Doctors or Physicians from the selected Hospital or Clinic



\-> Users select from the days of the month where the Physician is going to be available



\-> Users select from the time of the day from their selection as a meeting time based on the preference of the Doctor and the Hospital/Clinic



\-> Users can double check their choice 



\-> Users see the final screen telling them their appointment is successfully booked



### **UX Decisions**



Several key UX decisions were made to enhance usability and emotional comfort:



***Soft onboarding***: avoids overwhelming users at entry



***Delayed sign-up***: reduces friction and increases conversion



***Calm visual design***: soft colors and rounded typography create a reassuring experience



***Clear information hierarchy***: highlights current pregnancy week and next appointment



***Minimal feature set:*** focuses only on essential actions to avoid cognitive overload



***Supportive microcopy:*** uses friendly, reassuring language instead of clinical tone





### **How This Design Improves User Experience**



**This design simplifies the pregnancy care process by reducing confusion, anxiety, and missed appointments.**



**By combining appointment booking with a personalized pregnancy timeline, users always know:**



**when their next checkup is due**

**what stage they are in**

**what actions to take next**



**The streamlined flow, clear guidance, and supportive tone help users feel more in control, informed, and confident throughout their pregnancy journey.**
